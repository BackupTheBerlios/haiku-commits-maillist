Sorry, but this makes absolutely no sense. Could you please revert this whole
change?
There is one single reason why there are companion host controllers (UHCI,
OHCI) integrated into UBS 2.0 controllers: the EHCI one _cannot_ handle the
low and fullspeed devices. It's a different protocol. You certainly won't get
a USB 1.1 device working this way. If EHCI would support them there would be
no real reason to write UHCI and OHCI drivers by now.
