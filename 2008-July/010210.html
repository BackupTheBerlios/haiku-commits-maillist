> Just curiosity, I thought RTTI wasn't allowed when using C++ in the kernel?

RTTI is fine in the kernel -- we dropped "-fno-rtti" ages ago and link 
against libgcc. Technically exceptions would work, too, but the general 
sentiment was, that exceptions probably introduce more danger than benefits 
(though, admittedly I was wavering when writing the KDL parser), hence we 
still explicitly forbid them via "-fno-exceptions".
