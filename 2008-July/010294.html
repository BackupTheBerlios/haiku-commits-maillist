BMenu::_ComputeLayout() calculates the layout also when the BMenu is a
BMenuBar (IOW: BMenuBar doesn't have its own method to do that). In
that case, if the resizing mode is B_FOLLOW_LEFT_RIGHT, the preferred
width of the menubar is the width of the parent view/window.
