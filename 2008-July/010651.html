> Why is libGL x86-only anyway ?

Because we currently build our libGL.so by merging BGL* classes, GLU, GLUT and GL API dispatcher. 
Only the later is built with target architecture dependency. Unfortunatly nobody ever tune it for any arch but x86. I'll love that. Mesa have some PPC and SPARC support too, but the former is(was?) Linux dependent and we don't support Haiku on SPARC. Yet. ;-)
