> Just curious, is it safe to ignore the return code from
> get_memory_map_etc? Is it possible for a team to be destroyed before
> all its IO requests are completed?

get_memory_map_etc() is called on locked memory. Theoretically, that 
should be always safe, though it isn't yet in our implementation. The 
team/address_space destruction would need to wait until the last 
unlock_memory() has been called on previously locked memory.
