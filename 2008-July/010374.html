Nothing should have changed really except that (local) APIC timers are used in
non-SMP configurations too now. In the SMP case this was already the case
since Dustin's timer patch was applied. If it has such an influence on drawing
speed, that would indicate that the APIC timer does not work as expected. You
could check that by removing the APIC timer from the sTimers array at the top
of "src/system/kernel/arch/x86/arch_timer.c".
