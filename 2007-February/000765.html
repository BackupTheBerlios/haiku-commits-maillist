Wow, localizing the command line is really one of the greatest achievements 
of the past few years. I suppose the next big breakthrough will be 
localized programming and scripting languages. I can't wait to see this 
translation of the launch() function in our Bootscript:

starte () {
	wenn [ -d "/stiefel/$1" ]
	dann
		"/stiefel/$1" &
		[ "$2" != "" ] && warteauf "$2"
		gibzurück 1
	sonst
		echo Es gibt kein "$1"
	nnew
	gibzurück 0
}
