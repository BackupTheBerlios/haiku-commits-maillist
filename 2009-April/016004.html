To complement what Urias already wrote -- build != image. Adding something 
to the build means that it's part of the jamfile tree, and the target is 
visible/known to jam anywhere in the source tree. Adding it to the image 
means that the target is additionally added in the HaikuImage script, but 
that wasn't done in this commit.
