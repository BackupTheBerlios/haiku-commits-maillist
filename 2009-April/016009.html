That's not really a good idea. I haven't actually tested it, but it's 
possible that both will try to handle the hardware, leading to 
unexpected results. It's not meant to be installed in parallel, we will 
fully switch to ata in the coming days.

> Are we ready to remove the ide driver and it's supporting ideinfo,
> idestatus programs?

So far I am running both of my main systems with the new ATA bus_manager 
successfully. I have also set up a few test machines with various 
hardware configurations. If it works on those as well and proves to be 
reliable, I will switch the bus_managers included in the image and 
remove the old one.

The supporting programs can then either be removed as well, or renamed 
and extended for the new ATA bus_mangaer. For example I'd certainly 
merge both into one and I'd print out different information of the 
infoblock and status.
