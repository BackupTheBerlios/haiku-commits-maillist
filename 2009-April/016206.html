Besides that I haven't seen an open source profiler that was so good that I would consider porting instead of rewriting it, this is not about profiling (yet) -- at least not in the "how costly are my functions" meaning. The input data are scheduling events (i.e. thread scheduled/unscheduled + cause). Analyzing those yields hard info on latencies, lock contentions, starvation issues, etc. That's really nothing one can get with a common profiler.
