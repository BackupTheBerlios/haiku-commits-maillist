Sorry Stippi, but I did not mean to diminish the value your effort, or 
that of others for that matter, nor to question your judgement on 
technical grounds. I was just trying to...

1) Give some words of support to someone who was feeling (or at least 
seemed) that his effort was not being appreciated.

2) Raise the flag on what I see as a temptation for replacing native 
stuff with ported solutions. As well integrated and as nice to have in 
the short run these ported solutions may be (which I agree they can be

problem of lack of resources. I would not expect newcomers to take upon
