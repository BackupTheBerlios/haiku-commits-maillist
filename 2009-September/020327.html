I think People (and StyledEdit et al) should mimic 
what Tracker does to an open folder when you move
it to Trash: it closes the window.

folder may not be around for much longer.

Keeping the People window and emptying it is a lot 
less clear, IMO.

Deleting a file you have open for editing does not
imply wanting to create a new one.
