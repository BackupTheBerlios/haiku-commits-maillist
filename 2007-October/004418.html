Hi!
Nice to see you working on bluetooth!
In the Wikipedia article on JSR 82 there is a mention of a specific
problem with the API. Is this something you are taking into account when
doing the Haiku API?
Here's the link and relevant quote:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Java_APIs_for_Bluetooth#Common_Problems
"When they wrote the specification they misinterpreted the requirement
to reserve low ports for future use. Instead they chose to block all use
of low ports. This means that any future standard service making use of
the reserved ports will not work. The first example of such a service is
HID. Bluetooth keyboards, mice and other input devices can not connect
to java programs running on bluetooth mobile phones because of this
mistake."
