thanks for your compliments.

> If i may, one question:
> Is it possible to extend the format
> to support some kind of animation?

To tell the truth, the format was not designed with anything regarding 
animation in mind. I have not thought about it and don't know how difficult 
it would be. It largely depends on what kind of animation one has in mind. 
I mean, it is pretty easy to add animation in the form that there is one 
self-contained icon per frame. It is something completely different to 
store animation data in the document model of a single icon. One should 
probably look how SVG implements this. Anyways, at the time, I needed to 
solve a specific problem, and the design does not go beyond that scope. If 
you are interested in discussing this on a "technical musings" kind of 
basis, I'm absolutely interested in that, but better in private or on 
another list. However, I hope you understand, any discussion in terms of if 
and how Haiku needs animated icons should probably happen at a later point 
in time. :-)

Best regards,
-Stephan
