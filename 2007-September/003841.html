The bitmap's bounds initialization looks a bit weird anyway:

	BRect bounds(frame);
	bounds.OffsetTo(0, 0);
	bounds.left = 1024;
	bounds.bottom = 767;
	BBitmap bitmap(bounds, B_RGBA32);

Even if it was "bounds.right = 1023", which is probably, what was intended, 
every member of the BRect would have been overwritten, which would make the 
four lines pretty much equivalent to just "BRect bounds(0, 0, 1023, 767)".
