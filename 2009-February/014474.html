>> You can change vmware to emulate es1370/es1371 which is a PCI chipset
>> that OpenSound does support.
>
> Ah. How do you do that? My haiku.vmx just says 'sound.present = "TRUE"'.

sound.virtualDev = "es1371"

> Maybe we should update http://haiku-files.org/files/haiku.vmx and
> haiku/3rdparty/vmware/haiku.vmx accordingly.

You can also turn on usb devices with

usb.present = "TRUE"

I also have a second disk that I put things on that I want to keep
when I recreate the haiku image.

ide0:1.present = "TRUE"
ide0:1.fileName = "spare.vmdk"
ide0:1.deviceType = "disk"
ide0:1.mode = "persistent"
ide0:1.redo = ""
ide0:1.writeThrough = "FALSE"
ide0:1.startConnected = "FALSE"
