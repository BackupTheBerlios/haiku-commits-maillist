> > does not
> > quit with an error. This fixes the build on R5 and should therefore 
> > fix bug

> 
> But will this also fix the issue of missing icons (when compiling in 
> R5)?
> Why is update_mime_info failing at all?

No it doesn't fix missing icons. The icons are in the resources, they 
just aren't put into the attributes. But a failing mimeset that breaks 
the build doesn't help with missing icons either. That's why I made our 
mimeset behave as the one in R5 and ignore the failing call.

The reason why update_mime_info() is failing is probably due to the 
BeOS not recognizeing the file as an application (since the FileType-F 
add-on has no app signature this is somehow correct). It therefore 
probably fails when setting the version info. As we are using the 
system libbe here we cannot actually fix this problem in our tree. We 
would have to use our own libbe_build as on non BeOS hosts to get this 
corrected.
