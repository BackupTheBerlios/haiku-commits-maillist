Stefano Ceccherini schrieb:
> 2008/9/25 Stefano Ceccherini <stefano.ceccherini at gmail.com>:
>> 2008/9/25 julun <host.haiku at gmx.de>:
> 
>>> also note that the ref filter is not owned by the file panel, we
>>> are leaking them too.
> 
> BTW: Are you sure about that?
> If I allocate the filters on the stack and pass their pointers,
> expander crashes.

I'm sure, but you can't create them on the stack since they are 
not copied. They pointer to the filter is simply reused in 
TFilePanel, see src/kits/tracker/FilePanelPriv.cpp and 
FilePanel.cpp ctor.

Also the BeBook mentions this:
http://www.haiku-os.org/legacy-docs/bebook/BRefFilter.html

So basically you need to keep the pointer around or check in the 
dtor:

if(filePanel->RefFilter())
	delete filePanel->RefFilter();
