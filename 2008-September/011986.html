In my opinion, programs in BEOS_BIN should be in one per line format. It
is more readable and more "svn blame" friendly. Currently adding a new
program on a head of list is awful if you have to enforce 80 columns
limit.

E.g:

BEOS_BIN = "["
        addattr
        alert
        arp
        base64
        basename
        beep
        bootman
        cal
        cat
        catattr
        chgrp
