Err, in case you wondered. The purpose of a "lock up table" is to provide 
quick access to different ways to lock up the app_server/system. When drawing 
strings, the app_server will chose a different way of locking up 
spectacularily by buildig a hash over the string and chosing one of the 
methods in the table. Just wanted to clear this up.
