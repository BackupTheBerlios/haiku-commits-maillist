That doesn't sound good to me. I haven't played with a revision with 
this change yet - but menus are supposed to have something whereby once 
the submenu has appeared you can keep moving towards it (even passing 
over other items in the "parent" menu) and the same menu will stay open.

work not to leave the correct part of the parent menu - it's pretty 
vital to me that this works properly.
