I think you need to provide more info :-). The driver is tested with quite 
a lot of real Wacom tablets and works in absolute mode of course. What do 
you get from usb_dev_info? What is the observed behaviour exactly? By 
default, Wacom tablets act as HID mouse device (like if the pen was the 
mouse, relative mode). Our usb_hid driver ignores HID devices with Wacom 
vendor id (0x056a IIRC). If the device has correct vendor ID and works at 
all, then our Wacom driver picks it up. Maybe it pretends to be a device 
which the driver thinks it supports but has never been tested properly. In 
the input_server team, there should be a thread with the name of the tablet 
that was recognized. Could you please check and provide all this info?
