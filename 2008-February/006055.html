I have participated in numerous localization projects, and I can say 
with a high degree of confidence that non-English users don't 
necessarily translate all the name apps. For example, you can expect 
apps like Pulse, CodyCam, Icon-O-Matic, etc., not to be translated, and 
non-English users will still be able to identify by their original name.
