On Mon, 25 Feb 2008 19:56:18 +0100, mmlr at BerliOS wrote
> Do not disable interrupt vectors for shared interrupts when more 
> than 99% are unhandled. Otherwise it brings more trouble than it 
> solves as it also disables the interrupt of other devices (most 
> often USB or network).

By the way: At least on my laptop the unhandled interrupts seem to be caused
by HDA. I checked the unhandled count and on (heavily shared) interrupt line
10 I got a quickly increasing count. After removing the HDA driver this number
is now fixed (12) which shouldn't be a problem.
I have ruled out USB to be the problem because although interrupt line 10 is
also used by USB, USB (both UHCI and EHCI) is also present on interrupt line
11 where I don't get any unhandled interrupts.
