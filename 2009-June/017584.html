Well, they were implemented on BeOS R5 and were *not* buggy there.
What especially is the problem?

I really like to plaxe the window always-on-top in a corner of the screen,
and then remove frame, menu and controls.

It was possible to toggle them all at once using the space key.
Certainly not the best key for a media player, but as this was
originally implemented for the tv app, it made sense.

I would like to keep this feature. perhaps we can remove the individual options,
and have a shortcut to toggle them all? I believe this is pretty usefull, especially
as you can move the window by clicking and holding the mouse inside the
video display (worked very well and smoth with BeOS R5, ugly broken in Zeta,
not sure about current state in Haiku).
