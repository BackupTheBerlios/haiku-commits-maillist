To allow userland access to this bus, I suggest we keep the pattern already used by usb_raw, scsi_raw, fw_raw, aka exporting an acpi_raw to exposed ACPI features to third-party userland "drivers". Hardware thermal & power monitoring, extra LEDs and multimedia keys, such things are now often bound to ACPI.

An /dev/bus/acpi/raw could exposed its API via control(), while the current /dev/bus/acpi/namespace entry feature merged into it.

Plus, that will expand our consistency on busses userland API pattern...
