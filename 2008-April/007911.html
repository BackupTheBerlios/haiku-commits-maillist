> Because for me I find it more readable having the operators on the

Well, we just inherited the rule from OpenTracker, but I can tell you 
that I like it more because you (as a European language speaker) start 
reading from the left - you immediately know the meaning of that row 
without having to look at the end of the previous line first. Plus, it 
makes multi-line terms much nicer to read through indentation.
Ie.
	Aaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaaa
	||	Bbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbbb
		&&	Cccccccccccccccccc

is nicer to read and parse than
	Aaaaaaaaaaaaaaa ||
		Bbbbbbbbbbbbbbb &&
			Cccccccccccccccc
