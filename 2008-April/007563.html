> >   system_info::cached_pages field directly).
> Can you clarify what you mean by this? Is the value returned by
> cached_pages not correct, or am I misunderstanding what you mean 
> here?

What I meant is that we need to track allocations that are in fact 
cached data to have an accurate picture of it. The number of free pages 
even include the inactive pages - but those are in fact cached pages, 
too (they could just be reused at any time).
Now, when you're using more memory, you see that the number of cached 
memory is reduced - which is because the computation is not correct, 
not because it's actually reduced in the system.
