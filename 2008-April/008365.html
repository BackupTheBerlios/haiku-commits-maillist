 From an end user POV, this is how I envision taking a screenshot in 
Haiku should work:

* Press PrtScreen button.
* Popup menu is displayed to allow selecting save format or cancel 
(separate app window is overkill).
* Screenshot is saved on the desktop: to make it easier to find than in 
the Home folder.
* Screenshot icon has thumbnail: to make it even more obvious.

Simple, powerful, unobtrusive; the BeOS/Haiku way. :)
