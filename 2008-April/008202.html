In the same thread, Linus explains why all-spaces is bad:

<http://kerneltrap.org/mailarchive/git/2007/10/16/345332>

I don't understand all he says, I don't know if it all adds up and makes 
sense. But I think we are doing just fine with our all-tabs rule. Since we 
do not use mixed spaces and tabes, it doesn't even matter if you set your 
tab width to 4 or 8 characters. If you set it to 8, all that will happen is 
that lines extend beyond the 80 char limit. Besides, we do not encourage 
deep indentations. Code is much easier to read if you keep the indentation 
levels low and as little nested as possible.
