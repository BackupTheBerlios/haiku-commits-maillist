least for feedback. In 99.8% of the cases, hiding the password is not 
useful, since there is no one standing behind your back when you type it 
in. Windows has this insanely stupid mis-feature of hiding your SSID 
password for example. Good luck typing in your 26 digit hex key... Mac OS 
has a much more elegant solution, there is a simple checkmark you can use 
to hide/unhide the password. I find that very adequat (either you are or 
you aren't in a situation where someone can look over your shoulders, the 
computer cannot know, so it should be up to the user).
