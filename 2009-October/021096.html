preview view has a view color of black. Whenever that is obscured
whatever was covered gets repainted in black. If the screensaver
doesn't paint over that it stays black. I guess a proper solution

screensaver frame and use that for repainting when needed. But it
sounds a lot more complicated and inefficient than this solution ;)
