If www.haiku-os.org didn't redirect to haiku-os.org I would be more
supportive of leaving the www in the about window. In fact maybe
haiku-os.org should redirect to www.haiku-os.org (I see more sites
doing that than what we do.) In that case it would make more sense as
that means www.haiku-os.org is where our site is located. But right
now haiku-os.org is where it is located, since www redirects there and
no one sees www.haiku-os.org very long in their browser.

If we add subdomains in the future (users.haiku-os.org,
dev.haiku-os.org, forums.haiku-os.org, etc) it makes more sense to add
the www for the main web-site. Right now I don't see any subdomains
being used.
