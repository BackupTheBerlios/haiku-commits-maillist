Licences: seperate issue to what I'm discussing. The one issue with licences
I know of is the one I mentioned, there may be others.

Patents: If you start by removing the known-to-be-patented-somewhere mp3
decoder, where do you stop though? Every other codec is patented - wheter
its claimed (Ogg *) that its not or not, some element of it is patented.
Should all status bars be removed because Microsoft has them patented? In
the US, virtually every facet of the standard user interface is patented to
one firm or another.

"The Right Thing" is to warn people to check with their own lawyer, and
that's about it.
