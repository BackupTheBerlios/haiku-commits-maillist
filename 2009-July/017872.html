> > started it's low battery alert sound. The ACPI module was turned on and I
> > suspect it received an event. The panic was something about interrupts
> > being turned off when they are not allowed to be or vice versa.
> 
> Yes exactly, also when I changed the brightness or press the blue fn key I
> got a panic.
> So don't call get_module when handle a interrupt?

Definitely not. In an interrupt handler you are not allowed to do anything 
which may have to block (acquiring locking primitives, using ports, 
allocating memory, etc.). If you have to do such a thing you should use a 
DPC mechanism.
