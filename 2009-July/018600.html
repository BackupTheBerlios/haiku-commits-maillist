"François Revol" <revol at free.fr> wrote:
> Hmm would this fix the weird behaviour of CL-Amp eq & playlist 
> windows?
> They come up not visible but actually mapped.
> so when you click inside they actually start drawing something...

Actually, it's the other way around. They worked before this change, 
and are broken again now.
We're obviously doing something very different from BeOS wrt minimize/
show/hide.

CL-Amp minimizes its windows before showing them, TrackerStatus is 
hidden, and gets a show/hide level of 2 when selecting "Hide All" in 
the Deskbar, but doesn't get minimized like all other windows.

I'm not yet sure why and how the app_server manages to differentiate 
between the two cases (when calling minimize, CL-Amp is hidden in more 
or less the same way as TrackerStatus is hidden when you minimize it 
via "Hide All").
In any case, I'll revert this change, and reopen the bug in search for 
a better fix.
