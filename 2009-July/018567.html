Let me throw into the discussion that there's already a BLayoutUtils::AlignInFrame() version that aligns a rect in a given rect. So the CenterIn() would be equivalent to:

window->MoveTo(BLayoutUtils::AlignInFrame(screenFrame, window->Size(), BAlignment(B_ALIGN_HORIZONTAL_CENTER, B_ALIGN_VERTICAL_CENTER)).LeftTop());

(Assuming that the missing {BWindow,BView}::Size() are introduced.)

In principle there's nothing that speaks against a shorthand in BWindow to invoke that.
