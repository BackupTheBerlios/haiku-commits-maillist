This looks strange. It seems more like an implementation of an "Adopt()" 
method. From an operator=(), I expect the locker to give up it's current 
lock, detach from it's current target, then acquire a new lock for the 
other lockers target. BTW, even in an "Adopt()", one would have to give up 
it's own lock first.
