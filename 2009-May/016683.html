While I'm at it, I just looked at the dead key stuff now, and I'm not 
so fond of the BMenuBar - any reason you don't use a BPopUpMenu that 
does not take over the label from its selected entry?

Also, it does not seem to work correctly with regards to modifiers. How 
do I select which modifiers I need to have pressed to make the dead key 
work? For example, in the American keymap, the '`' key is available via 
the "option" modifier as dead key, but is a standard key when you don't 
press that modifier.
I am not sure if that would be the perfect solution, but we could make 
the contents of the popup menu depend on the current modifiers, and 
also disable keys that aren't accessible with the current set of 
modifiers.
