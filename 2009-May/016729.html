might present themselves before alpha 1. I figured it would be a good idea 
to avoid inflationary introduction of Haiku version macros and 
corresponding compatibility handling in the runtime loader -- i.e. only 
have those for actual releases -- but it might be a good idea to

consolidate those when feature-freezing for the release, requiring 
rebuilding of all optional packages at that point.
