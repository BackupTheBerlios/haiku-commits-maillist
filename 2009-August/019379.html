There are a few arguments for using extensions with data files besides the mentioned 
exchange with users of other platforms and serving stuff on the net:
  * Users expect to see their ".mp3" files
  * More importantly, MP3 players etc. want their .mp3, .jpg, .avi
  * In (Mini) Icon mode you won't have the Kind attribute displayed
  * A ls in Terminal leaves you clueless

So, it's not _only_ Windows' braindeadness...
