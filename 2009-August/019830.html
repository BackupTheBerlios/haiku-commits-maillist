OK, I didn't know the conditions for when the window would be resized,
though that certainly makes sense. It is probably better to let the
window have explicit control over its size anyhow (at least beyond
what B_AUTO_UPDATE_SIZE_LIMITS does.)

handler for B_LAYOUT_WINDOW. This seems to work for the two current
test apps.
