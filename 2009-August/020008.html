I always cringe a bit when I read "R123456". The thing is, that we 
usually use lower case "r" to designate revisions, so "r123456" is 
revision 123456 in our repository. Upper case "R" on the other hand is 
used for "Release" as in "R1" or "R1/alpha1". The lower case "r" will 
also trigger automatic linking in our bugtracker. So instead of 
"R123456" or "rev. 123456" or similar writing "r123456" will 
automatically link to the changeset in question when you write that in 
a bugtracker ticket, which makes things easier.
