Now, setting aside which keys to actually use for the shortcuts, I think having an 
in/decrease mechanism is much better than assigning absolute values to different keys.
  * You can have as many in/decrements as you want,
  * with an arbitrary step-size,
  * without squandering precious shortcut keys.
  * You always know what to press to in/decrease something: "At what zoom level are we? 
ALT+4 or ALT+5..?"
  * Having two keys is easily mappable on multifunctional keyboards with dedicated +/- keys.

Taking the + and - keys for that is just very memorable.
Could the "unmodifiered" keys of different keymaps be included as shortcut, e.g. if "-" is 
on SHIFT+6, have ALT+6 do the same as ALT+SHIFT+6 or ALT - ?
