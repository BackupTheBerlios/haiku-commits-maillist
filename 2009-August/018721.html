http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tooltip). Changing the name of the contants 
from *TOOLTIP* to *TOOL_TIP* does not make much sense (and, as François

tooltips are an welcome addition. In fact, maybe we should automatically 
add one to text that is automatically truncated and have ellipsis added?
