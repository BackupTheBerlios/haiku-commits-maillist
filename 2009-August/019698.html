> * Switch over the default scheduler to scheduler_affine().

The idea was to auto-select the scheduler depending on the number of CPUs. 
There's no point in suffering the additional overhead of the affine 
scheduler, if there's only one CPU. That also means the simple scheduler 
should be stream-lined for that purpose, of course.
