> Make mountvolume a BApplication and set it as the

It's fun to play around with. The volumes show up in
Tracker's mount menu. Moving a mounted volume's image
to Trash appears to unmount it.

Mounting a set of images serially works nicely, as
happens when doing Open With > mountvolume on a 
selection of images.

Mounting images concurrently does show problems.
This happens when you Command-O a set of image files
in Tracker and multiple mountvolume get launched.

I don't know if the issue is in mountvolume or in the
kernel, but I don't think my changes have much to do
with it, except making it easier to see happen.
