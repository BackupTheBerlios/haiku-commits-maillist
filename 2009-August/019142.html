> 
> Would you explain how you do this?

Add "mountvolume Data" as the first line of Bootscript :) Nothing fancy, 
really. But it does not work anymore dur to the changes in mountvolume. 
The alternative is adding, alkso as the forst lines, this:

mkdir /MountPointName
mount /path/to/partition /MountPointName

Note it is the system Bootscript. Not UserBootscript.

Then my /boot/home is a symlink to /MountPointName/home. QED. :)
