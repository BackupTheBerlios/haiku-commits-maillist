The indexed attributes are the soul of Haiku and its file system; by 
saying what you are saying, in a way you are negating one of the (strong 
IMO) selling points of Haiku. Relegating BFS attributes to second class 
citizenship as you suggest not only would you make Haiku less unique and 
interesting, but you open the door to making that selling point less 
compelling, probably even irrelevant over time.
