> Would these changes have an effect on socket descriptors as well? I've
> noticed select() doesn't always seem to properly detect socket
> exceptions here in the past, but always assumed it was just
> incompleteness in the network stack.

The changes also affect sockets, as they are just FDs, but only at the functionality level that is provided by the VFS. I.e. close() on a socket should now emit a POLLNVAL event, unless the underlying socket implementation does emit some other event on close already. POLLHUP/POLLERR should not be affected at all.
