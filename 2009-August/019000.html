Focus policy comes into play too, when scripting, as a
window may avoid getting focus back from Screenshot when
it hides to take the shot. Workspaces is like that, which
is probably why it doesn't get transparency. A workaround
for this could be to script all windows for their rects
and compare that to the one from window_info.

Or simply extend window_info as suggested. :-]

(Not knowing much about the app_server internals, 
what populates and maintains window_info, I didn't
feel confident to do so yesterday.)

Another [unrelated] case where transparency fails is when
a window tab is partially offscreen.
