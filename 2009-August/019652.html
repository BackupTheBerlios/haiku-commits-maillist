Since BMessage is just a container for typed data, I'd vote for using the 
rdef format which has a similar purpose. We also have resattr which 
converts between resources and attributes. Another tool could add the 
conversion to and from flattened BMessages. Ideally all four formats could 
be supported by a single library/application.
