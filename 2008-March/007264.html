Mmh, I keep asking myself: Why queries? Gathering security-relevant files from 
all over the disk doesn't strike me as particulary secure. It can't be for 
performance reasons either, since, I'm sure, doing the query will be slower than 
reading the info from a file (as long as one doesn't have thousands of 
users/groups anyway -- but then a query-based initgroups() will suck big time).

Unless the general sentiment is in favor for queries, I'll go ahead and implement 
the traditional single-file-based method.
