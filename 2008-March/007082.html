Some notes, now that I tried to build the cross tools on some other 
machine: It will not work out of the box under BeOS as both a current 
texinfo and autoconf are necessary. Texinfo is not the problem, one can 
easily get it to work under BONE at least. But autoconf would again 
require perl which I didn't have handy on the other machine. I remember 
now that I once ported over autoconf 2.6 and texinfo 4.9 on my primary 
machine, which is why everything worked fine for me there. The autoconf 
package on BeBits is not current enough to build the cross tools it 
seems.
