I'm with Ingo on this one. The precedence of common operators should 
really be known - the knowledge of their precedence cannot degrade over 
time, as you are using them all the time.

And I know for certain that additional parenthesis makes parsing harder 
for me; your mileage might differ. When there are parenthesis, I expect 
a reason for them, and it takes me longer to see that there is no 
reason for them at all than to just understand the common precedence 
rules.

I agree that the rules for <<, >> might be confusing sometimes, and 
then I can surely understand and support the idea of adding redundant 
parenthesis.
