Good to hear (that it works and that I don't have to fix the rest ;-). The
errors evaluate to 0x04 = stall and 0x05 = no response. Both are common ways
for a device to say "operation/feature not supported". If this happens during
init and the driver handles it correctly this shouldn't pose a problem. HID
devices will for example often stall the "get hid descriptor" call while they
succeed for "get report descriptor", therefore usb_hid simply ignores the
failure of the former (don't know if that's specs compliant though). If the
device otherwise works as expected this should be fine.
