For now I am starting with something simple: a command-line
application that takes a screenshot when invoked. The BWindow keyboard
handler will just Launch that with be_roster. There will be no passing
of bitmaps around.

I have a simple version working already.

I figure I can add options for what part of the screen to capture,
what format to save in, and a delay before taking the screenshot. The
defaults will be the full screen in PNG with no delay. If the person
holds shift when pressing Print Screen only the current window will be
captured (by sending it's frame as the frame to capture.) This does
not capture the decorators though, but that is for another day I think
:)

I may add some kind of notification that a screenshot was taken, but
that is probably it for now.
