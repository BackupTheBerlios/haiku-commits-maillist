I think the original reason for them being uint8 instead of uint16 is that the
source of the values, the class_base, class_sub and class_api fields in the
pci_info struct in PCI.h, are uchar. Maybe it was changed to uint16 in the
device manager to support other busses that provide corresponding 16bit
values. Since the output here specifically are PCI variables it might make
sense to actually use uint8?
